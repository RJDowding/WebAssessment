21 Crimes Dunnottar Castle
21 Crimes is one of the most exclusive bars in Aberdeen. A throwback to the classic speakeasies to which members must know the constantly changing passphrase to gain entry. The bar is a nod to the penal colonies of the British Empire, where criminals were extradited to colonies if they committed one of the '21 Crimes'
