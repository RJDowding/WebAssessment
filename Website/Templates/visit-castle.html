21 Crimes Dunnottar Castle
Dunnottar Castle is located 30 miles south of the city, outside the small town of Stonehaven. The Castle is build right at the edge of the North East shoreline, perched atop a 160ft rock sat at the cliffs edge. The fort was originally built for Earls Marischal, one of the most powerful families in Scotland. Standing for over 1000 years, the castle has been home for many important figures in Scottish history from William Wallace to Mary Queen of Scots.
